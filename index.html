About

I'm Pratyush Nayak — a second-year B.Tech CSE student at CUTM University, specializing in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning. Based in Bhubaneswar & Balasore, Odisha.

I don't just study tech — I build with it. My flagship product Tpixel AI is an Indian personal voice assistant that uses real AI (Claude API + Web Speech + Indian TTS) to serve real users. I've shipped full websites for solar and trading businesses, built freelance Java backends, and I'm constantly leveling up.
